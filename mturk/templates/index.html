Tag fashion photos

General Instructions:

This task has two parts. You will be shown a fashion photo.
In Part One, you will be selecting the appropriate tags based on the entire outfit or the model.
In Part Two, you will be selecting the appropriate tags based on the individual clothing items shown in the photo.
Detailed instruction and references are provided as you progress through the task.

Notes:

Please remember to accept the task before you do it; otherwise, we cannot pay you!

The task starts below the line

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You should see the top and bottom of an outfit on a single model. If you don't see the full body of the model or see anything else, please reject the image.

Instruction for Part Two: tag individual clothing items

We are now ready to start Part Two of the task. You will be tagging individual clothing items shown in the picture.
Every outfit consists of a top and/or a bottom. For example: a T-shirt (top) and jeans (bottom). Sometimes an outfit just has a top, like a dress. Sometimes an outfit has an outer-wear layer like a jacket or a vest.

Step 1:

Draw a box around the top of the outfit, and then select the appropriate tags for the top. Sometimes an outfit has a Top AND an Outer-wear (jacket, coat or a sweater), select the Top in this step for now, you can select the Outer-wear later.

Step 2:

Draw a box around the bottom of the outfit, and then select the appropriate tags for the top.

Step 3:

If the outfit has an outerwear layer, draw a box around it and tag appropriately.

Step 4:

If the outfit has shoes, draw a box around them and tag appropriately.

Step 5:

If the outfit has a handbag, draw a box around it and tag appropriately.

In this task we need you to identify the individual clothing items by drawing boxes around them, then tag the individual clothing items accordingly.

Congrats! The end of task! Please submit!
